A birthday pub crawl

You've heard of Chase the Chicken — but introducing the Rat Race. A birthday pub crawl where the birthday girls dress as rats, take everyone's cash and scurry off into the depths of Angel. Your job? Hunt them down. Every wrong pub costs you a drink or challenge, and the sooner you find them, the more of the pot you get to drink. Meet at 3PM for pres, collect the pot, and let the race begin.

Georgia & Alysha. They put on the ears. They take the pot. They get a 20-minute head start. They scurry off into Angel and drink for free — for as long as it takes everyone to find them.
