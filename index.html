All the Details

Your questions, answered

📍 Where exactly is 14 Gaskin Street? ▼
A 7 min walk from Angel tube station. Head up Upper Street, turn left on Gaskin St. → Open in Google Maps
🏠 How do I get into Casey & Tom's? ▼
14 Gaskin St, Angel, N1 2RU 1. Head to the entrance of Gaskin St and find the black gate
2. Press the green button with the key icon and come through
3. Once inside, buzz apartment 3 (door on your right) on the intercom
4. We're on the first floor — go straight ahead through the door
🍽️ Should I eat before? ▼
Yes — you should have some lunch before you arrive. Light snacks at Casey & Tom's and you can order some dinner at Canonbury Tavern from 6:30PM.
🍺 Should I BYO drinks? ▼
Yes! BYO is welcome for pres at Casey & Tom's from 2PM. Once the race starts you'll be buying at pubs.
🐀 What is a Babybel and why does my team have one? ▼
Each team gets a Babybel as their mascot. You decorate it at pres, your team must protect it all day and feature it in several challenges. Do not lose your Babybel... or else.
📱 Location sharing — yes or no? ▼
Turn it OFF. No location sharing with other teams.
🤝 Can I swap teams? ▼
You may not swap teams before the race starts — stay together and don't split up. After 30 minutes, if you run into another team, one member from each team must swap.
🍹 What drinks can I order? ▼
Every member of your team must order a drink at each pub. Water or soft drinks are allowed. Shots count as a drink (if you want).
⏰ What happens at 6PM? ▼
Race ends at 6PM regardless. Pub revealed to anyone who hasn't found the rats. Everyone heads to Canonbury Tavern, 21 Canonbury Place, N1 2NS for 6:30PM.
🧀 Questions on the day? ▼
Message Casey or Cortnee. Don't ask the rats — they're in hiding. 🐀

How to Play

The Rules

01
The Pot
Transfer £20 to Alysha before the day. Birthday girls take it, hide with it, and drink on it all night.
02
The Rats Disappear
Georgia & Alysha get a 20-minute head start. They pick any pub in the Angel chase zone. They go there. They sit. They drink. They cannot move once chosen.
03
The Chase
Teams fan out. Every pub you enter and the rats aren't there — every player must finish a whole drink before leaving (alcoholic or non-alcoholic — shots count!). No sneaking out early.
04
No Transport
Walking only. No Lime Bikes, no buses, no taxis. If it has wheels, it doesn't count.
05
Stick Together
Teams must stay together at all times — no splitting up. No swapping teams once assigned.
07
Locations Off
Turn location sharing OFF for the entire race. No sharing your location with other teams.
08
Team Swap
After 30 minutes: if you run into another team, one member must swap. The opposing team gets to choose who they steal.
09
Clues
The rats will issue clues to the group chat based on how many teams have found them. More teams find them = fewer clues remaining.
10
Race Ends at 6PM
Pub revealed to all remaining teams. Everyone converges on Canonbury Tavern by 6:30PM.
